## Becoming a Maintainer

spanforge is actively looking for co-maintainers. If you have:
- Made 3+ meaningful contributions (code, docs, or community support)
- Demonstrated understanding of the RFC-0001 schema design
- Shown good judgement in code reviews

…please open a GitHub Discussion titled "Maintainer nomination: \<your GitHub username\>" and introduce yourself.

Existing maintainers will review nominations and reach consensus within 4 weeks.

## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Triage and respond to issues within 5 business days
- Review PRs and provide actionable feedback within 7 days
- Participate in at least one quarterly roadmap review
- Follow the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) and enforce it fairly
- Keep the release process documented and executable by any maintainer
